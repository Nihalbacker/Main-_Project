Parameters Used Description
Time (year-month-day h:m:s) Timestamp indicating the specific date and time when the measurements were taken.
Wind Speed at Height of 10 Meters (m/s)The speed of the wind measured at a height of 10 meters above ground level, in meters per second.
Wind Direction at Height of 10 Meters (˚) The direction from which the wind is blowing at 10 meters above ground, measured in degrees.
Wind Speed at Height of 30 Meters (m/s)Wind speed recorded at 30 meters above the ground.
Wind Direction at Height of 30 Meters (˚)The wind direction at 30 meters height.
Wind Speed at Height of 50 Meters (m/s)The speed of the wind measured at 50 meters above ground level.
Wind Direction at Height of 50 Meters (˚)Wind direction measured at 50 meters above the ground.
Wind Speed at the Height of Wheel Hub (m/s)Wind speed measured at the height of the turbine’s wheel hub, critical for assessing the actual wind energy available to the turbine.
Wind Direction at the Height of Wheel Hub (˚)Direction of the wind at the turbine wheel hub’s height.
Air Temperature (°C)The temperature of the air at the time of measurement in degrees Celsius.
Atmosphere (hPa)Atmospheric pressure at the time of measurement, in hectopascals.
Relative Humidity (%) The percentage of moisture in the air compared to what the air can hold at that temperature.
Power (MW) The power output being generated from the wind energy source, measured in megawatts.
